My vacation reading. After the first book I thought I will not finish the second part but here we are. It was not bad, it was not remarkable either. It was fine fantasy reading with not much that would stay with me. The metaphysics multiverse was kind of confusing and maybe not necessary? I like the aesthetics of the world. If you liked the Mordie, you will like this as well.
